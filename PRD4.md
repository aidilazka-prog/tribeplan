### Feature 4: Trip Setup & Onboarding
- **Role System:** The first user to create a trip is the "Trip Leader."
- **Setup Screen:** Leader enters the Trip Name (e.g., "Bali 2026") and creates a simple "Join Password."
- **Member Creation:** Leader can pre-add member names (e.g., Mom, Dad, Kirana) so they appear in the expense/itinerary dropdowns immediately.
- **Shareable Link:** Generate a unique URL (simulated for V1) that the leader can copy and send to others.
- **Member Access:** When members open the link, they enter the "Join Password" to gain access to the shared dashboard.
